Job Portal

Project description

Job portal is a job-related search engine for job listings. This website is mainly used for job search and apply for jobs. Here people can register themselves, login with their credentials and apply for jobs or post jobs according to their profile.

Users:

Employers: Employers will be the company people where they will present their respective company with some company details.

Job seekers: Job seekers are people looking for jobs where they find jobs according to their experience and qualifications.

Admin: Admin will the user who will be looking to all things if anything is inappropriate(offensive data) admin will be there to manage.

My features:

Company reviews: Here people can drop reviews on company they have worked before or currently working.

Statistics: Here job seekers can compare salaries with different company which company is offering most and what are their expectations.

Project Link

Challenge

To build this kind of applicaton we faced many challenges like:

  • Legimate registration
  • Login
  • Add company reviews
  • Edit and delete reviews who are authenticated

Solutions

Users can only register with registered email address. When user register themselves user will receive an email from job portal website, where user will receive a link to activate link when a user click on it user will be activated to job portal website.

Only after activating user will be able to login into the system.

Adding reviews without login into system is quit not possible because if user is not logged in user wont be able to edit or delete their review. After login into system user will be able to add, update or delete their reviews.

Comparing salaries and statistics doesn't require any authentication because it doesn't involve any user data or personal informations.
