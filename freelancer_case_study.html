Freelancer

Project description

Freelancer website concept was taken from freelancer website where a person having skills can earn from home. Where people looking for work can earn money or people wants to do some side business can do it. Where a freelancer can bid on projects and on the other side employers or people who post work or projects can see bid and choose the person they want to give project to. Where Freelancer can bid with cost and duration and employer can choose which is better for employer company or himself or herself.

Users:

Employers: Employers will be the company people where they will present their respective company with some company details and post projects on website.

Freelancer: Freelancer will be the person wants to work from home. They can bid on projects and when they get the project work on it and deliver it.

Admin: Admin will the user who will be looking to all things if anything is inappropriate(offensive data) admin will be there to manage.

My features:

  1. Registration/Login
  2. Posting projects
  3. Bidding
  4. Communication
